Enrolling in a club¶

Staff can add patrons to any club¶

  1. Look up a patron through the checkout or the patron search tool
  1. If there are clubs available at your library, you’ll see a “Clubs” tab on the checkout and details pages
  1. When you click on that tab, you’ll see a list of the clubs available at your library
  1. Click on “Enroll” to add that patron to the club
  1. Answer the enrollment questions
  1. Click on “Finish enrollment”
  1. The patron is now enrolled in this club

Self-enrollment via the OPAC¶

  1. The patron logs into their account in the OPAC
  1. If there are clubs available at the patron’s home library, on the “Your summary” page the patron will see a tab for “Clubs”
  1. When the patron clicks on that tab they will see a list of clubs they are members of as well as any self-enrollment clubs availale at their home library
  1. The patron neds to click on “Enroll” to join a club they are not already a member of
  1. The patron needs to answer the enrollment questions
  1. The patron needs to click on “Finish enrollment”
  1. The patron will now see the club they just joined in their list of clubs they are currently enrolled in
